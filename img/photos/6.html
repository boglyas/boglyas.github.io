20ft Shipping Container - 6m x 2.4m

20ft Storage Container Hire- shipping containers are the ideal site storage shed, easily secured and the perfect way to organise your tools, equipment and materials on a construction, demolition or civil work site.

20ft containers are one of the most popular sized globally for transportation and storage. Featuring double door entry that can fit 2 standard pallets side by side.

Hiring a shipping container can be more than just storage, it can also be the best way of transporting your materials, tools and equipment to and from your next job.
